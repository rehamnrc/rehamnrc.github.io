Definition of the Norwegian Council

The Norwegian Refugee Council (NRC) is a Norwegian non-governmental humanitarian organization dedicated to assisting people and human rights in countries hosting refugees or internally displaced persons such as the Middle Eastern countries where events such as Syria and others took place.

Definition Of Youth Center

It contributes to the development of youth and youth by investing their free time in the practice of various cultural, social, sports and national activities. It seeks to acquire the skills that guarantee responsibility in the framework of law and public policy. The National Project for the Development of Youth Centers is implemented in order to transform youth centers into productive centers of a developmental nature that provide various cultural and social services and play an active role in the development of prevailing concepts and values.
