How will you evaluate?

You will receive a report at the end of the course that includes your grades in Conversation, Listening, Reading and Writing skills, as well as scores on your participation, behavior, and effort during the class. If you pass the course you will be awarded a certificate and move to the next level. If you do not, the teacher may suggest that you repeat the course to make sure you are learning with people of the same level as you.
